1. **Constants:**
   - `FILAMENT_DENSITY: Record<string, number>` — pla: 1.24, petg: 1.27, abs: 1.04, asa: 1.07, petgcf: 1.30, petcf: 1.35, pacf: 1.40 (g/cm3)
   - `DEFAULT_FILAMENT_PRICES: Record<string, number>` — pla: 20, petg: 22, abs: 24, asa: 24, petgcf: 35, petcf: 40, pacf: 45 ($/kg)
   - `DEFAULT_FILL_FACTOR = 0.12` — calibrated against typical rack panel geometry (hollow interior, wall loops, infill, cutouts)
   - `SHEET_METAL_RATE_PER_CM2: Record<string, number>` — crs16: 0.07, crs18: 0.06, al14: 0.11, al16: 0.10 ($/cm2 material+laser)
   - `FABRICATOR_URLS = { sendcutsend: 'https://sendcutsend.com/', protocase: 'https://www.protocase.com/price/instant-quote-rackmount.php' }`

2. **Interface `CostEstimate`:** `{ low: number; high: number; central: number; assumptions: Array<{ label: string; value: string }> }`

3. **`estimatePrintCost(input: PrintCostInput): CostEstimate`** — Pure function.
   - `PrintCostInput`: panelWidth (mm), panelHeight (mm), enclosureDepth (mm), fillFactor (number 0-1), materialDensity (g/cm3), pricePerKg ($/kg), materialName (string)
   - Volume: `(panelWidth/10) * (panelHeight/10) * (enclosureDepth/10)` for cm3
   - materialVolume = boundingVolume * fillFactor
   - mass = materialVolume * materialDensity (grams)
   - central = (mass/1000) * pricePerKg
   - low = central * 0.75, high = central * 1.25 (fixed +/-25% band per CONTEXT.md)
   - assumptions array: Bounding volume, Fill factor %, Material mass, Price/kg, Material name

4. **`estimateSheetMetalCost(input: SheetMetalCostInput): CostEstimate`** — Pure function.
   - `SheetMetalCostInput`: flatWidth (mm), flatHeight (mm), ratePerCm2 ($/cm2), materialName (string)
   - area = (flatWidth/10) * (flatHeight/10) for cm2
   - central = area * ratePerCm2
   - low = central * 0.75, high = central * 1.25
   - assumptions array: Flat pattern area, Rate/cm2, Material name, "Material + laser cut only (no bends)"
